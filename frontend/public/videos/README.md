# Construction Site Video

## Instructions

1. **Add Your Video File Here**
   - Place your construction site video in this folder
   - Recommended filename: `construction-site.mp4`
   - Supported formats: MP4, WebM, OGG

2. **Video Specifications**
   - **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 1280x720 (HD)
   - **Duration**: 10-30 seconds (will loop automatically)
   - **File Size**: Keep under 10MB for faster loading
   - **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)

3. **Video Content Suggestions**
   - Drone shots of construction sites
   - Workers operating machinery
   - Cranes and excavators in action
   - Construction progress timelapse
   - Building construction scenes

4. **Where to Get Videos**
   - **Free Sources**:
     - Pexels Videos: https://www.pexels.com/videos/
     - Pixabay Videos: https://pixabay.com/videos/
     - Unsplash Videos: https://unsplash.com/videos
   - Search for: "construction site", "building construction", "construction workers"

5. **After Adding Video**
   - The video will automatically play as background
   - If video doesn't load, animated fallback will show
   - Video loops continuously
   - Muted by default (required for autoplay)

## Example Video URLs (for testing)

You can also use external video URLs in `Login.jsx`:
- Replace the source tag with your video URL
- Make sure the video allows embedding
